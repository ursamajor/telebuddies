How does this work?

  • Input your major. TeleBuddies connects your major requirements to your courses.
  • Input your previous courses. What classes have you taken previously? TeleBuddies matches your major to classes that you've taken before to find the perfect match.
  • Input what you're looking for. Whether you're looking for machine learning, economic development, or the history of English literature; we'll be sure to find something for you.
  • Find your courses!

Discover new classes.

We go through our database of courses and the past schedules of students using our application to recommend courses to you based on your own preferences!

Plan solid back-up courses.

TeleBears is never reliable. Use TeleBuddies to find back-up courses just in case if your number one schedule doesn't work out and you don't know what else to take.
